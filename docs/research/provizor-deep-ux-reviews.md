### ИТ-Аптека — Analytics Pain Points (itapteka.ru articles)

Key findings from expert articles:
1. **Managers don't use the pharmacy POS system** — they need a separate analytics layer
2. **Cloud BI with 100+ metrics** is the proposed solution
3. **Benchmarking against market standards** is a requested feature
4. **Drill-down** from chain → pharmacy → category → SKU is essential
5. **Accessible from any device** (mobile-first for managers)

### М-Аптека — Inventory as Financial Risk

Key finding: **Inventory costs often exceed payroll** as the biggest expense category. Yet most pharmacies don't track inventory efficiency metrics (DOI, turnover, dead stock %).

## Synthesis — White Spaces & Opportunities

### Confirmed White Spaces (0 of 7 competitors do this)

1. **Multichannel margin analysis** — offline vs Halyk Market vs Wolt vs iTeka. No competitor tracks per-channel profitability. This is Provizor's primary differentiator.

2. **CIS Profitability %** (рентабельность вложенных средств) — the FarmBazis formula exists in their educational content, but NO product surfaces it as a dashboard metric.

3. **Per-SKU channel profitability** — which specific drugs lose money on which marketplace, accounting for commissions.

### Validated Needs (competitors partially address)

4. **Dashboard for non-technical managers** — managers don't use POS systems. Need separate analytics layer. SmartApteka and Heado partially address, but neither has financial modeling.

5. **Defectura tracking** — М-Аптека has it, Опора doesn't. Gap in 3 of 7 products.

6. **Benchmark comparison** — ИТ-Аптека proposes it, but no product fully implements pharmacy-specific benchmarks with RAG indicators.

### Anti-patterns to Avoid

7. **Never lock in customer data** — Опора's biggest pain point. Always allow full data export.
8. **24/7 support or self-service** — support unavailability = pharmacy closure.
9. **Modern UI, not 1С yellow** — users actively complain about 1С aesthetics.
10. **No unnecessary steps** — every click costs time at the counter.
