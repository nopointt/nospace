## 1. What Bailiffs CAN Seize

### Bank Accounts
**All bank accounts** in the debtor's name are subject to seizure. Bailiffs (ЧСИ — частные судебные исполнители) impose an arrest that prohibits the owner from disposing of funds within the arrest amount.

**2025 changes:** Bailiffs can no longer "zero out" accounts completely — a minimum living amount must remain. Social payments are protected.

Source: [yur.kz — Арест счетов 2025](https://yur.kz/articles/arest-schetov-i-imushhestva-v-kazahstane-izmeneniya-2025-goda-i-prava-dolzhnikov/)

### Brokerage Accounts & Securities
**Yes, can be seized.** Статья 65 Закона об исполнительном производстве explicitly covers ценные бумаги (securities). Brokerage accounts are treated as property. Arrested securities are not physically seized but operations are frozen.

Source: [bailiff.kz — Статья 65](https://bailiff.kz/zan/article65/)

### Crypto on Exchanges (KYC-linked)
**Likely can be seized** under new legislation (2025-2026). President Tokayev signed laws in January 2026 regulating "uncovered digital assets" (cryptocurrencies). The government has created a **national crypto custodial service** based on the Central Depository and plans to replenish a **strategic cryptocurrency reserve** with seized assets.

In practice: if your exchange account is KYC-linked to your ИИН (personal ID), bailiffs could potentially request a freeze. **Unconfirmed** whether bailiffs currently have automated access to exchange databases (like they do with banks).

Source: [zakon.kz](https://www.zakon.kz/sobytiia/6505837-dvoe-astanchan-obmenyali-kriptovalyutu-na-7-mlrd-tenge-i-lishilis-svobody.html), [probusiness.news](https://probusiness.news/probusiness/korporativny/novyy-poryadok-izyatiya-kriptovalyuty-v-ugolovnykh-delakh-s-2026-goda)

### Real Property, Vehicles, Other Assets
Standard enforcement — all property in debtor's name is subject to seizure except protected categories.

## 2. What Bailiffs CANNOT Seize (Protected)

### Special Accounts (Спецсчета)
The following accounts are **immune from seizure**:
- Social payments and benefits (пособия, соцвыплаты)
- Housing savings (жилищные строительные сбережения)
- Educational savings (образовательные накопительные вклады)
- Alimony for children (алименты)

To activate protection: open a special account at any bank, mark it with "специальный счёт" designation specifying the payment type.

Source: [informburo.kz](https://informburo.kz/cards/na-kakie-scheta-sudoispolniteli-ne-mogut-nalozhit-arest), [fingramota.kz](https://fingramota.kz/ru/news/post/imeyut-li-pravo-chastnye-sudebnye-ispolniteli-blokirovat-neprikosnovennyj-bankovskij-schet)

### Minimum Living Amount
Since 2025 changes, bailiffs must leave minimum funds for survival. Exact amount: tied to прожиточный минимум.

### Pension Accounts (ЕНПФ)
**⚠️ UNVERIFIED** — likely protected but no explicit source found. Needs verification.

## 3. Self-Custody Crypto — Can Bailiffs Reach It?

**Legally:** A court can issue a seizure order for cryptocurrency. The order must identify the amount and the address owner.

**Practically:** Seizing self-custody crypto requires the owner's **private key**. Without cooperation from the debtor, enforcement is extremely difficult. Law enforcement globally has much greater success seizing crypto from exchanges (where the exchange holds keys) than from self-custody wallets.

**Kazakhstan-specific:** No evidence found that Kazakhstan bailiffs have tools or procedures to seize self-custody crypto in civil enforcement proceedings. Criminal cases (like the 7 billion tenge exchange case) involve law enforcement, not civil bailiffs.

**Key risk factor:** The new national crypto custodial service (Central Depository) could change this landscape. If assets are held through regulated custodians, they become reachable.

**Bottom line:** Self-custody crypto (hardware wallet, seed phrase) is currently the **most enforcement-resistant** asset class, but not legally exempt.

Source: [chainalysis.com](https://www.chainalysis.com/blog/cryptocurrency-asset-seizure/), [river.com](https://river.com/learn/can-bitcoin-be-seized/)

## 4. AIFC — Astana International Financial Centre

### Dual Regulatory Framework (2026)
Kazakhstan now has a **dual supervisory framework**:
- **National Bank** — decides which digital assets can be traded on licensed exchanges
- **AIFC/AFSA** — licenses and regulates crypto platforms within the AIFC jurisdiction

### Market Scale
Trading on AIFC-licensed platforms surged **20x** — from €270M (2023) to nearly **€6B** (first 9 months of 2025). The market reached **$6.8 billion**.

### Licensed Platforms in AIFC
14 platforms with AFSA licenses: Bybit, Binance, Gate.io, OKX, HTX, BingX, MEXC, KuCoin, Bitget, Phemex, Garantex, CoinW, Bitpapa, Toobit.

### Relevance for Nomos
AIFC platforms are **legal** for retail crypto trading. BUT — they require KYC (linked to your ИИН). The question is whether bailiffs can reach AIFC-regulated exchange accounts. AIFC operates under English common law, but enforcement cooperation with mainland KZ courts is likely.

Source: [astanatimes.com](https://astanatimes.com/2025/12/kazakhstans-crypto-market-surges-to-6-8-billion-as-aifc-expands-regulated-ecosystem/), [euronews.com](https://www.euronews.com/business/2026/02/11/kazakhstan-is-positioning-itself-as-eurasias-next-crypto-hub)

## 5. Crypto Tax for Individuals in Kazakhstan

- **Rate:** 10% on profit (разница между покупкой и продажей)
- **Declaration:** Form 270.00, deadline September 15 of the following year
- **No ИП required** for occasional sales
- **New Tax Code 2026** — first detailed regulation of crypto taxation
- **Key:** Only the profit (capital gain) is taxed, not the total sale amount

Source: [mybuh.kz](https://mybuh.kz/useful/torgovlya-kriptovalyutoy-v-kazakhstane-kakie-nalogi-platit.html), [buh.mcfr.kz](https://buh.mcfr.kz/article/1562-tsifrovye-aktivy-i-kriptovalyuta-v-nalogovom-kodekse-2026-poryadok-ucheta-i-nalogooblojeniya)

## 6. Bankruptcy — Path to Debt Freedom

### Three Procedures
1. **Внесудебное банкротство** (extrajudicial) — debt under 1,600 MCI (~6.3M KZT)
2. **Судебное банкротство** (judicial) — larger debts or when property exists
3. **Восстановление платежеспособности** — solvency restoration

### Extrajudicial Bankruptcy (Most Relevant)

**Conditions:**
- Debt under 1,600 MCI (~6.3M KZT in 2025, ~$12,000)
- No payments for 12 months continuously
- No property besides единственное жилье (primary residence)
- No bankruptcy in last 7 years
- Applied to bank/MFO for resolution attempt

**Exceptions (easier path):**
- Receiving адресная социальная помощь for 6+ months → the 12-month no-payment requirement is waived
- No payments for 5+ years → debt amount and property requirements don't apply

**Timeline:** 6 months officially, often 2-3 months in practice

**Application:** Through egov.kz portal

**Reality check:** 87% rejection rate (only ~34K of 270K applications approved as of 2025)

**Cost:** Filing is free through egov.kz. ⚠️ UNVERIFIED — there may be fees for judicial bankruptcy. Needs verification.

**Consequences:** Cannot file again for 7 years. Credit history impact.

Source: [primelegal.kz](https://primelegal.kz/details/bankrotstvo-fizicheskih-lic-kazahstan-2026-kak-spisat-dolg), [egov.kz](https://egov.kz/cms/ru/services/economics/pass_P1_mf), [moneypanda.kz](https://moneypanda.kz/kz/news/kak-podat-na-bankrotstvo-v-kazakhstane-instruktsiya-2026-goda-817)
