### 5.2 Anchor Text Distribution Formula

Healthy anchor text profile for a new SaaS domain:
- **Branded** (exact brand name: "Contexter", "vault.contexter.cc", "contexter-vault"): 40-50%
- **Naked URL** (raw URL as anchor): 20-25%
- **Partial match** (e.g., "RAG as a service", "Claude Code proxy"): 15-20%
- **Exact match** (target keyword verbatim): MAX 5-10% — above 20% triggers penalty risk
- **Generic** (e.g., "here", "click here", "learn more"): 5-10%

Sites in Google's top position average 13% exact-match anchors. New domains should stay below that until DR50+.

Source: [EMGI Anchor Text SaaS Link Building](https://emgigroup.com/blog/anchor-text-saas-link-building/), [BluTree Anchor Text Optimization](https://bluetree.digital/anchor-text-optimization-backlinks/), confidence: HIGH.

### 5.3 Domain Authority Distribution Targets (12-month)

Healthy DR mix for a growing SaaS at end of year 1:
- DR70+ (Product Hunt, G2, Crunchbase, major tech publications): target 10-15% of referring domains
- DR40-70 (niche SaaS directories, developer newsletters, security blogs): target 25-35%
- DR<40 (smaller blogs, community sites, niche directories): 50-60%

Aiming for 50 total referring domains in 6 months is achievable with this plan.
