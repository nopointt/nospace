## Detailed Profiles

### 1. GrowthFactor — Closest Global Analog

**URL:** growthfactor.ai
**Market:** US franchise & retail chains
**Pricing:** from $400/mo (Small Business Starter, <10 locations), $1,000-2,500/mo mid-tier

**What they do:**
- Centralized financial and operational data from individual franchise/retail locations
- Standardizes data → actionable insights about profitability, costs, performance at unit level
- Multi-unit performance dashboards
- Unit economics tracking per location
- Territory mapping + site selection analytics
- Competitive analysis
- Predictive modeling for new location performance

**AI features:**
- Predictive site scoring (forecasts new location performance from historical unit data)
- Anomaly detection (flags unusual performance patterns before critical)
- Natural language query interface (non-technical users ask questions of data)

**Results:**
- 4% profit margin improvements through benchmarking
- New locations selected with GrowthFactor outperform by 15%

**Relevance to Provizor:** HIGH — same mental model (per-location P&L, benchmarking, multi-unit comparison). But:
- US-focused, no KZ regulatory knowledge
- No pharmacy domain expertise
- No channel margin analysis (offline vs marketplace)
- No CIS Profitability formula
- $400+/mo pricing excludes 5-pharmacy chains in KZ

### 2. TrueProfit — Best-in-class UX for profit tracking

## Key Takeaways for Provizor

### 1. We occupy a unique intersection

No existing product combines:
- Per-location P&L (GrowthFactor's strength)
- Real-time profit dashboard UX (TrueProfit's strength)
- Pharmacy domain expertise (SmartApteka's strength)
- KZ regulatory compliance (nobody)
- Multichannel margin analysis (nobody in pharmacy)

### 2. Pricing reference

- GrowthFactor: $400-2,500/mo (US market, 10-500 locations)
- TrueProfit: $25-100/mo (Shopify, per-store)
- SmartApteka: ~$100/mo (Russia, per-location)
- **Provizor target range: $50-150/mo per pharmacy** (KZ market, 5-pharmacy chain)
- At 5 pharmacies: $250-750/mo total — competitive vs GrowthFactor, premium vs SmartApteka

### 3. Feature inspiration from global leaders

From **GrowthFactor:**
- Anomaly detection (alert when metrics deviate)
- Benchmarking across locations
- Predictive modeling for new location decisions

From **TrueProfit:**
- 5-star UX simplicity
- Auto-sync with data sources (1С equivalent)
- Product-level (SKU-level) profitability

From **Drivetrain:**
- "Spreadsheet-like UI with plain English formulas" — could be powerful for pharmacy managers who think in Excel

### 4. Market gap confirmed globally

The global landscape confirms:
- **Retail unit economics platforms exist** (GrowthFactor, Retlia) — but not for pharmacy
- **Pharmacy management systems exist** (SmartApteka, PioneerRx) — but not for unit economics
- **Nobody serves small pharmacy chains (<20 locations) with unit economics analytics**
- **Nobody serves KZ/CIS market with modern web-based pharmacy analytics**

### 5. SaaS pricing for meeting with Alimkhan

When discussing pricing with Alimkhan:
- "Global analogs charge $400-2,500/mo for similar analytics"
- "We start at a fraction of that, localized for KZ"
- Value prop: "One insight about an unprofitable channel saves more per month than the subscription costs"
