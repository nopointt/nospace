## Synthesis Note for Batch 11

This batch contains no new theoretical principles — it is the reference apparatus and physical container of the book. Its value for the Bauhaus Code project is documentary:

1. **The Sachregister provides the complete terminological inventory** of Moholy-Nagy's theoretical vocabulary, with frequency data. The highest-frequency terms (faktur, konstruktion, material, raum, licht, biologisch/biotechnik, gleichgewicht, durchdringung, funkionsbegriff) constitute the actual operative core of the book's argument, regardless of chapter organization.

2. **The Namenregister confirms the intellectual network**: Picasso is the most extensively cited single artist (74–87, 90, 110, 129, 138, 187 — eight page ranges). Gropius is cited in the architectural synthesis chapters. The Bauhaus colleagues (Klee, Kandinsky, Schlemmer, Itten) appear at specific points. The constructivists (Tatlin, Lissitzky, Rodschenko, Gabo, Pevsner, Malevich) are clustered in the volume chapter.

3. **The Bauhausbücher catalogue confirms that *Von Material zu Architektur* is Volume 14** — the final volume of the series. Its position as the last book is significant: the series moves from architecture (Vol.1) through painting, workshops, and theory, and culminates with Moholy-Nagy's pedagogical synthesis. The book is literally the summation of the Bauhaus project in book form.

4. **The library circulation record (1957–1976)** confirms sustained scholarly use of this book well into the postwar period, indicating its continued relevance beyond the immediate Bauhaus context.
