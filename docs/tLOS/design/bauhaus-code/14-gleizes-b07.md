## BATCH IDENTIFICATION NOTE

These pages (PDF 0-indexed 120–139) are NOT from the original 1928 Bauhaus-Bücher #14 "Kubismus." The PDF in question is the **1964 Guggenheim Museum exhibition catalogue** for "Albert Gleizes" (Exhibition 64/5, September–October 1964), published by The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation and printed by Joh. Enschedé en Zonen, Haarlem. The documentation section begins on p. 120 and constitutes the entire remaining content of the PDF.

This batch contains **no theoretical text, no principles, no diagrams, no painting reproductions with analytical commentary, and no pedagogical exercises.** It is the documentary apparatus of the retrospective exhibition: exhibition history, bibliography, museum staff, photographic credits, and touring venue information.

All items below are documented faithfully. No theoretical extraction is possible from these pages.
