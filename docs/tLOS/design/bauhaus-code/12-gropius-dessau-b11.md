## Siedlung Dessau-Törten — Urban Housing Settlement (p.199)

### Argument Structure
**Thesis**: The housing settlement at Dessau-Törten demonstrates that Rationalisierung (rationalization) extends beyond individual buildings to entire urban ensembles — the same principles of serial production, standardization, and functional site planning that govern a single house govern the layout of a neighborhood.
**Evidence**: Aerial-perspective photograph from the main road showing the linear housing rows with the Konsumverein Dessau building (cooperative store) prominent at the eastern terminus; the street infrastructure and the buildings read as a single designed system.
**Conclusion**: The Siedlung is not incidental background to the Konsumverein — it is proof that Bauhaus principles of Rationalisierung produce coherent urbanism, not merely objects.

### Principles

**PRINCIPLE [P-01]**: Rationalisierung als gesellschaftliche Bewegung / Rationalization as a social movement
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The drive toward rationalization originates in the economic sphere — industry rationalizing production, the labor market accelerating exchange of workers — but its consequences reach into the transformation of the entire built environment and human life. The text on p.200 states that rationalization, emerging from the economic life of peoples, has become a civilizing force that changes people's relationship to the world.
- **Rule**: Treat rationalization not as a stylistic choice but as an irreversible civilizational force; design must align with it, not resist it.
- **Source**: p.200
- **Quote (DE)**: "der gedanke der rationalisierung ist vom wirtschaftsleben der völker ausgehend zu einer großen geistigen bewegung in der zivilisierten welt geworden; sie hat zu einer veränderten lebenseinstellung geführt, die neue schöpferische kräfte auslöst."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The idea of rationalization, originating in the economic life of peoples, has become a great spiritual movement in the civilized world; it has led to a changed attitude to life that releases new creative forces."
- **Category**: function

**PRINCIPLE [P-02]**: Rationalisierung ist nicht mechanische Ordnung / Rationalization is not mechanical order
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: It would be an error to believe that the goal of rationalization in construction is purely social improvement by mechanical means. Rationalization is not a mechanical ordering principle — the ratio of the individual must not be forgotten in the process.
- **Rule**: Rationalization must serve the human individual, not subordinate them; the person's ratio (reason, proportion, individuality) remains the non-negotiable measure.
- **Source**: p.200
- **Quote (DE)**: "es wäre ein irrtum, zu glauben, daß das ziel der rationalisierung der bauwirtschaft allein darin liegt, das menschliche werde basisökonomisch zu verbessern; die rationalisierung ist nicht eine mechanische ordnung; wir dürfen um keinen preis über der ratio das individuelle vergessen!"
- **Quote (EN)**: "It would be an error to believe that the goal of rationalization in construction lies solely in improving the economic base of humanity; rationalization is not a mechanical ordering; we must not at any price forget the individual in the ratio!"
- **Category**: pedagogy

## Arbeitsamt Dessau — Employment Office, 1928–1929

### Argument Structure
**Thesis**: The Dessau Employment Office (Arbeitsamt) demonstrates that functional form directly solves organizational problems invisible to conventional rectangular planning — the semicircular plan is not a formal gesture but the only adequate spatial response to the building's operational program.
**Evidence**: Full program description (p.202 text); construction specifications; floor plan (Abb. 188); four elevations and section (Abb. 190–194); site plan and axonometric (Abb. 189); construction photographs (Abb. 195–196); interior photographs (Abb. 197–200); exterior photographs (Abb. 201–203).
**Conclusion**: Plan geometry is program geometry. The semicircle eliminates bottlenecks at the central cashier, distributes entrances to the periphery by occupational group, and places the consultation rooms at the perimeter where they can receive natural light — a complete functional-spatial argument realized in steel, glass, and brick.

### Principles

**PRINCIPLE [P-03]**: Halbkreisform als Funktionslösung / Semicircular form as functional solution
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The Employment Office serves large numbers of job-seekers from multiple occupational sectors simultaneously. The semicircular plan distributes the peripheral entrances (one per Berufsgruppe) around the arc, leads all visitors through a segmented waiting hall toward the central cashier core, and places consultation rooms at the inner perimeter. The form eliminates queuing bottlenecks (Stauungen) at the single cashier by radiating all circulation paths toward one focal point. The plan caption explicitly states: "erdgeschossgrundriss zu ebener erde zur vermeidung von stauungen an treppen" (ground floor plan at ground level for the avoidance of congestion on stairs).
- **Rule**: When a program requires simultaneous access by many people from multiple categories, use radial or fan geometry that converges flows to the service point without producing a single bottleneck corridor.
- **Source**: p.204 (Abb. 188), p.202 (text)
- **Quote (DE)**: "erdgeschossgrundriss zu ebener erde zur vermeidung von stauungen an treppen"
- **Quote (EN)**: "Ground floor plan at ground level to avoid congestion on stairs"
- **Category**: function

**PRINCIPLE [P-04]**: Eisenskelettbau mit Vorhangfassade / Steel skeleton with curtain wall facade
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The Arbeitsamt is built as a steel skeleton (Eisenskelettbau). The entire exterior masonry (Verblendsteine in white-glazed brick) is cladding, not load-bearing. Window strips in steel profiles run as horizontal bands. The flat roof over the rectangular administration wing is covered with cork-pressed sheeting (Korkestrich). The structure is completely separated from the skin.
- **Rule**: In a steel skeleton building, the facade is always a Vorhangfassade — hung from the frame, not structural. Express this in the window-to-wall ratio: continuous horizontal bands, corners freely turned.
- **Source**: p.202 (text), p.207 (Abb. 190–191), p.208 (Abb. 192–194)
- **Quote (DE)**: "der flachbau ist ein eisenskelettbau. das gesamte mauerwerk ist mit lederfarbenen verblendsteinen bekleidet. das flachdach des verwaltungshauses mit kiespresdach auf korkestrich gedeckt. sämtliche fenster bestehen aus stahlprofilen."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The low building is a steel skeleton building. All masonry is clad with leather-colored facing bricks. The flat roof of the administration building is covered with gravel-pressed sheeting on cork sub-floor. All windows consist of steel profiles."
- **Category**: material

**PRINCIPLE [P-05]**: Shedbau für diffuses Nordlicht / Shed-roof construction for diffuse north light
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The semicircular waiting hall is covered by a Shedbau (sawtooth roof construction) whose north-facing glazed slopes provide uniform, glare-free diffuse light to the entire interior. The shed profiles lean against the semicircular flat structure; the skylights use Riffelglas (ribbed/corrugated glass) for further diffusion. Frameless glazing (kittlose Oberlichtsverglasung) is used throughout the shed roof panels.
- **Rule**: For large public halls serving detailed tasks requiring even illumination (reading, filing, consultation), use Shedbau with north-facing glazing; Riffelglas eliminates direct sunlight penetration while maximizing light quantity.
- **Source**: p.202 (text), p.203 (Abb. 187 caption), p.208 (Abb. 194 section), p.212 (Abb. 199)
- **Quote (DE)**: "an den halbkreisförmigen flachbau lehnt sich der zweiegeschossige, für das publikum in der hauptsache nicht zugängliche verwaltungsgebäude an. die halbkreisform hatte zur folge, daß die belichtung der im innern liegenden räume mit halbkreisförmig angeordneter shedreihen erfolgte."
- **Quote (EN)**: "Against the semicircular flat building leans the two-storey administration building, which is largely inaccessible to the public. The semicircular form resulted in the illumination of the interior rooms being achieved with semicircularly arranged shed rows."
- **Category**: function

**PRINCIPLE [P-06]**: Berufsgruppen-Segregation an der Peripherie / Occupational-group segregation at the periphery
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: Each occupational category (Berufsgruppe) has its own entrance at the periphery of the semicircle. This means job-seekers enter directly into their designated waiting segment without crossing other flows. The segregation at the perimeter produces order inside without requiring partitions or internal circulation control.
- **Rule**: When a program serves multiple distinct user categories, segregate their entry points at the building's perimeter; internal space can then remain unified and flexible.
- **Source**: p.203 (Abb. 187 caption), p.204 (Abb. 188 plan), p.217 (Abb. 203 caption)
- **Quote (DE)**: "nordwestansicht mit den an der peripherie liegenden eingängen für die verschiedenen berufsgruppen"
- **Quote (EN)**: "Northwest view with the entrances for the various occupational groups situated at the periphery"
- **Category**: function

**PRINCIPLE [P-07]**: Terrazzo und Bauhaus-Werkstätten als Materiallieferanten / Terrazzo floors and Bauhaus workshops as material suppliers
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The floor surfaces of the public and waiting areas are natural-colored terrazzo with brass dividing strips (Messingbänder). The Bauhaus joinery workshop (Bauhausschreinerei) supplied the furniture, the Bauhaus metal workshop (Bauhaus-Metallwerkstatt) supplied the lighting fixtures, and the Bauhaus interior finishing workshop supplied the signage and interior fittings. The Arbeitsamt is not only a building by Gropius but a comprehensive Bauhaus output.
- **Rule**: A building project is complete only when furniture, lighting, signage, and all fittings are designed within the same system as the architecture; engage workshop production from the start of design.
- **Source**: p.202 (text)
- **Quote (DE)**: "die böden der inneren und verwaltungsräume sind naturfarbener steinholzestrich. der warteräume terrazzo mit messingteillbändern. die bauhausschreinerei lieferte die möbel, die bauhaus-metallwerkstatt die beleuchtungskörper und die bauhausausbau-werkstätten übernahm die farbige gestaltung."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The floors of the interior and administrative rooms are natural-colored stone-wood composition. The waiting room floors are terrazzo with brass dividing strips. The Bauhaus joinery workshop supplied the furniture, the Bauhaus metal workshop the lighting fixtures, and the Bauhaus interior finishing workshops took charge of the color design."
- **Category**: workshop-integration

**PRINCIPLE [P-08]**: Kostenargument als sachliche Rechtfertigung / Cost argument as objective justification
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: Total construction cost: 297,950 RM. Cost per cubic meter of enclosed volume: 39.9 RM. Total built area: 1,555 qm. Total enclosed volume: 7,461 cbm. Gropius provides these figures directly as evidence that functional design is economical — the sachlicher Stil produces buildings that are also cost-effective, not luxurious extravagances.
- **Rule**: Always provide cost data alongside design arguments; economy is not the enemy of quality but its proof.
- **Source**: p.202 (text)
- **Quote (DE)**: "die kosten einschließlich aller nebenkosten betrugen 297950 rm, oder 39,9 rm pro cbm umbauten raum."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The costs including all ancillary costs amounted to 297,950 RM, or 39.9 RM per cubic meter of enclosed space."
- **Category**: function

**PRINCIPLE [P-09]**: Der Architekt als sozialer Organisator / The architect as social organizer
- **Statement** [CERTAIN]: The goal of the architectural profession is that of a unifying organizer — one who, proceeding from the socially valid conceptions of life, collects all scientific, technical, economic, and formal-design problems of building and fuses them into a unified work through collaborative work with numerous specialists and workers.
- **Rule**: Architecture is not the expression of individual genius but the disciplined coordination of all technical, economic, and social forces toward a unified result; the architect is a coordinator, not a solo author.
- **Source**: p.216 (text)
- **Quote (DE)**: "das ziel des architektenberufes ist das eines zusammenfassenden organsisators, der von sozialen, d. h. im sinne der gemeinschaft gültigen, lebensvorstellungen ausgehend alle wissenschaftlichen, technischen, wirtschaftlichen und gestalterischen probleme des bauens zu sammeln und in gemeinschaftsarbeit mit zahlreichen spezialisten und arbeitern planvoll zu einem einheitlichen werk zu verschmelzen hut."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The goal of the architectural profession is that of a comprehensive organizer, who, proceeding from social — that is, communally valid — conceptions of life, collects all scientific, technical, economic, and formal-design problems of building and fuses them purposefully into a unified work through collaborative work with numerous specialists and workers."
- **Category**: pedagogy

**PRINCIPLE [P-10]**: Variable Trennwände im Beratungsbereich / Movable partitions in the consultation zone
- **Statement** [INFERRED]: The inner perimeter of the semicircle houses individual consultation rooms (Einzelberatungsräume) for male and female job-seekers, separated by movable partitions (verschiebbare Trennwände). This allows the space to be reconfigured according to the daily and weekly fluctuation in demand for different advisory services.
- **Rule**: Where program demand fluctuates across time, use movable internal partitions rather than fixed walls; structure defines the zone, partitions define the moment.
- **Source**: p.202 (text)
- **Quote (DE)**: "die lösung hat nach dem weiteren umstand, daß dem schwankenden raumbedarf für die männlichen und weiblichen beratungsstellen durch verschiebbarkeit der trennungsschränke im inneren umgang variable entsprochen werden kann."
- **Quote (EN)**: "The solution takes into account the further circumstance that the fluctuating space requirements for the male and female consultation points can be variably met through the movability of the partition screens in the inner corridor."
- **Category**: function
