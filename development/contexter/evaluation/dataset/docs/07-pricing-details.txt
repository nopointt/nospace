Free Usage:

Refund Policy:
  Cryptocurrency payments are non-refundable due to the nature of blockchain transactions. Users can downgrade or cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Cost Structure (our costs per user):
  At 10,000 users with DeepInfra: approximately $0.075 per user per month.
  Primary cost driver: LLM inference (Groq/DeepInfra) at ~93% of total API costs.
  Server: Hetzner CAX41 at €30/month scales to 10,000 users.
