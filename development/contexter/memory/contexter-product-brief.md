## 1. Positioning

**Tagline:** One memory. Every AI.

**Positioning statement:**
For knowledge workers who use multiple AI tools, Contexter is one memory that works across every AI you use — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity. Upload your documents and conversations once, and every AI already knows your work. Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, which lock your knowledge inside their walls, Contexter keeps it yours.

**Category:** AI memory for everyone (not "RAG platform", not "context engine", not "knowledge base API")

**Two killer features:**
1. Connects to ALL LLMs — not locked to one model
2. Any format, any size — PDF, audio, video, images, no limits

**Key differentiator:** Built for non-technical people. Every competitor is developer-first.

### Section 10: FAQ / Objection Busters

**Q: Is my data safe?**
Your files are only accessible to you. We don't use your documents to train AI, don't share them, don't sell your data. What you upload stays private.

**Q: Do I need to be technical?**
No. If you can attach a file to an email, you can use Contexter. Nothing to install, no code, no complex settings.

**Q: How is this different from uploading to ChatGPT?**
When you upload to ChatGPT, the file exists for one conversation. Close the chat — it's gone. Contexter keeps everything. Upload once — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini all access it anytime.

**Q: What formats work?**
PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, audio (MP3, WAV), video (MP4), images (JPG, PNG), plain text, Markdown, and more. No file size limits for normal business documents.

**Q: Is it free?**
Yes, there's a free plan. No credit card needed to start.

## 7. Copy Rules (for anyone writing Contexter content)

1. **NEVER use:** RAG, embeddings, vector, MCP, API, chunks, pipeline, context window, tokens, self-hosted
2. **ALWAYS use:** remembers, knows, forgets, memory, your stuff, every AI, all your AIs
3. **Tone:** Human, calm, slightly warm. Not corporate, not hype.
4. **Reading level:** 5th-7th grade. Short sentences. Common words.
5. **Name competitors:** ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini by name. This is social proof + answers "does it work with my tool?"
6. **"Seriously"** and **"Совсем"** — use when making a claim that sounds too good ("no technical knowledge required. Seriously.")
7. **RU is not a translation of EN.** Write natively. "Все нейросети" not "все LLM". "Наконец-то" carries weight.
